Pro Bono Website Design for Local Nonprofits

Alfred Web Design & Shirts is offering our design and development services pro bono to build a 3-page website for qualifying nonprofits, community initiatives, and local charities in our area. This offer is designed to help small organizations establish a clean, professional online presence.

A Partnership in Success

To ensure the long-term security, speed, and success of your new website, we require grant recipients to partner with us on a heavily discounted first-year maintenance plan, starting at just $99. This investment secures your spot and covers essential services like hosting, security scans, and expert support.

What's Included in the Grant

  • 3-page static website (Home, About, Contact or similar)
  • 1 simple contact-style form (up to 10 fields)
  • Mobile-friendly, responsive design
  • Basic SEO optimization
  • Free basic hosting
  • FREE domain (.com, .net, or .org — new or transferred)
  • "Designed by Alfred Web Design" footer credit

Required Maintenance Plans

Your investment in one of these plans serves as your commitment and covers all your first-year running costs.

Basic

$99/year
  • 2 hours of support per year
  • Quarterly security scans
  • Annual site checkup

Standard

$199/year
  • 4 hours of support per year
  • Monthly security scans
  • Domain email forwarding setup
  • Annual site checkup

Premium

$349/year
  • 12 hours of support per year
  • Monthly security scans
  • Annual strategy session
  • Annual site checkup
  • 1 custom social media graphic per year

Content changes are billed at 15 minutes per item (text or image).

Support hours do not roll over. Additional work is billed at $50/hr.

After the first year, organizations can renew, upgrade/downgrade, or cancel their plan, or purchase the site through our buyout option. Hosting is only available with an active maintenance plan or after buyout.

Organizations are eligible to apply for a site redesign after 3 years.

Requirements to Qualify

  • Must be a registered nonprofit, charity, or community cause
  • Must agree to:
    • Provide a Google Review or testimonial
    • Allow portfolio showcase
    • Include footer credit
    • Permit social media shout-out
  • Must select and pay for a maintenance plan within 14 days of acceptance
